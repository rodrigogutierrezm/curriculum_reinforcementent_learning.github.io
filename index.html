Rodrigo Gutiérrez Moreno

Postdoctoral Researcher at RobeSafe Research Group

Rodrigo Gutiérrez Moreno received his BSc degree (2012-2017) in Industrial Electronics and Automation Engineering, from the University of Alcalá and his MSc (2017-2019) in Automation and Robotics, from the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. He received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering in 2024 from the University of Alcalá, Alcalá de Henares, Spain. He is a member of the RobeSafe research group. His research interests include robotics and control, autonomous driving techniques, decision-making algorithms, artificial intelligence, and sim2real methodologies. He has authored more than 20 publications in international journals and conference proceedings.
